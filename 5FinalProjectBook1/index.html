KOREAN VITAMIN VOLUME 1

RM 79.90

LEVEL: BEGINNER

DESCRIPTION:-

VITAMIN KOREAN IS A NEW METHOD TO LEARN KOREAN FROM THE VERY BEGINNING. THIS IS THE FIRST VOLUME. THIS METHOD IS DESIGNED TO LEARN THE FOUR LANGUAGE SKILLS THROUGH THEORETICAL CONTENT WRITTEN IN ENGLISH AND LOTS OF EXERCISES. THE FIRST TWO VOLUMES OF THIS SERIES CONTAIN TRANSLATIONS AND TEXT IN ENGLISH, HOWEVER, FROM VOLUME 3 ONWARDS, TEXTS ARE WRITTEN ONLYIN KOREAN. AT THE END OF THE BOOK, STUDENTS WILL FIND THE ANSWER KEY TO THE EXERCISES, AUDIO TRANSCRIPTIONS, GRAMMAR EXPLANATIONS IN KOREAN AND GLOSSARY WITH VOCABULARY. EVERY LESSONS, THERE ARE EXERCISES FROM THE OFFICIAL TOPIK EXAM. THIS BOOK INCLUDES LOTS OF PICTURES AND THE DESIGNED HAS BEEN CAREFULLY MADE. THIS METHOD IS GREAT FOR THOSE WHO WANT TO LEARN KOREAN AND AT THE SAME TIME, THEY WANT TO PRACTICE TO TAKE THE OFFICIAL TOPIKEXAM.
